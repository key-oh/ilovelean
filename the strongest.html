Satoru Gojo

Who is Satoru Gojo?

Satoru Gojo is a major character in the manga and anime series "Jujutsu Kaisen." He is the most powerful sorcerer and a teacher at the Tokyo Jujutsu High. Known for his charismatic personality and immense strength, he possesses rare & extraordinary abilities, including the Six Eyes and Limitless Cursed Technique. His iconic blindfold and striking blue eyes make him one of the most recognizable characters in the series.
